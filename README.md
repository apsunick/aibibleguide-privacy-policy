 # AI Bible Guide Privacy Policy

  **Last Updated:** November 2, 2025

  ## Overview
  BibleGuide ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your
  information.

  ## Information We Collect

  ### Account Information
  - Anonymous user ID (Firebase Anonymous Authentication)
  - Subscription status and purchase receipts (via Apple's App Store)
  - AI usage data (interaction count for billing purposes)

  ### AI Interactions
  - Your questions and AI responses are processed by OpenAI
  - Conversations are not stored permanently
  - We do not sell or share your AI interactions with third parties

  ## How We Use Your Information
  - To provide AI-powered Bible study features
  - To manage your subscription and usage limits
  - To improve our services
  - To comply with legal obligations

  ## Data Storage
  - User data is stored securely in Firebase (Google Cloud)
  - AI requests are processed by OpenAI's API
  - Apple processes all subscription payments

  ## Data Sharing
  We share data only with:
  - **OpenAI**: To process AI requests (subject to OpenAI's privacy policy)
  - **Apple**: For in-app purchase processing
  - **Firebase/Google**: For backend services

  We do NOT sell your data to third parties.

  ## Your Rights
  - You can delete your account data by uninstalling the app
  - You can cancel your subscription anytime in iOS Settings
  - Contact us for data deletion requests

  ## Children's Privacy
  Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children
  under 13.

  ## Changes to This Policy
  We may update this policy. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of changes.

  ## Contact Us
  For privacy questions, email: support@retireplanai.com

  ## Bible Content
  Scripture is from the World English Bible (WEB), which is in the public domain.
